ABOUT[001]

Hello, I'm Tanvi, a frontend developer specializing in creating user-friendly and visually stunning websites for the digital realm.

My journey as a frontend developer commenced during my 9th grade when I fortuitously encountered developer tools. Initially perceiving them as mere entertainment, I remained oblivious to their true potential. However, this encounter kindled a profound fascination within me, propelling me towards the path of web development

Fast forward to the present, I have sharpened my skills and expertise, fully prepared to translate my passion for frontend development into a prosperous career. Blending aesthetics and functionality, my approach aims to create captivating websites with seamless and intuitive user experiences. I meticulously craft every element, from layout and typography to responsiveness and interactivity. Thriving on new challenges, I actively contribute to impactful projects. If you are seeking a dedicated frontend developer, let's connect and collaborate to build extraordinary web experiences together.
